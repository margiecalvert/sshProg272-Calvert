Margie Calvert's Midterm

Be sure to choose Read All before you do anything else. If no data comes through, click "Insert Data in Database" and try again.

Click "Clear List." This will allow you to visualize short lists of poems. Then choosing "Find Fairest" will return all poems containing the word fairest. Choosing "Find Tyrant" will return all poems containing the word tyrant. You can choose to see the text of the poem you want by entering the number in the field labeled "Show Sonnet" below the area where the poem will display.
